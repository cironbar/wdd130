White Water Rush Adventures

Our mission is simple: to connect people with the river's wild spirit through safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences. We are committed to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 1995 by a crew of veteran river runners, White Water Rush was born from a passion for the great outdoors and a desire to share the thrill of the rapids. We started small, but our dedication to quality guiding and client satisfaction quickly established us as the premier outfitter in the region.
